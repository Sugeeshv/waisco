META

Variable speed pressure boosting system with integrated control

Execution

Self-priming booster set with built in frequency converter.

MÈTA is a plug and play solution, the pump is equipped with an integrated pressure transducer, an integrated check valve and a built-in pressure vessel. The Vsd controls the start and stop of the pump and allows to keep a constant pressure.

Boosting sets with 2 pumps Suction and delivery manifolds in stainless steel AISI 304.

Connections for the installation of one G 1″ connection pressure vessel.

Applications

For water supply systems.

For domestic use, for garden use and irrigation.

Operating conditions

Liquid temperature: 0 °C to +35 °C.
Ambient temperature up to 40° C.
